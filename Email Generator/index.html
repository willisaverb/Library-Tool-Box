LibraryGO Letter Generator

How to use this tool:

  1. Select the appropriate letter template from the dropdown.
  2. Enter the customer's first name to personalize the letter.
  3. Click "Generate Letter" to prepare the text.
  4. Use the copy buttons to quickly grab the subject and body.
  5. Paste into your email client and you're ready to send!
